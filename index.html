About

Born 6/11/67 Scott Millard Atherton aka Buz Bomb started his entertainment career in 1984 playing with band after band. In 1996 he finally went solo and has released countless albums, most of which we're recorded from 2013- 2019. His other ventures include music videos, web series, podcasting, live performance, film directing, production, editing, acting, promotion and booking. This is his second published book the other being 'They Call Her Christina'. Now out of print, it gave a glimpse into what his writing talents encompass. With a Bachelor's Degree in the visual and performing arts, his experience and learning have played a major role in in his success. This book is the culmination of over a years work. It started as a screenplay but his writing style lent to it being in a book format. So a book it has become. Scott has a 20 year old son from a previous marriage. He lives with his wife and two adult children. Hailing from Port Jefferson Station, New York, he has spent his entire life on Long Island. The book encapsulates a story line that is made to engage the reader, leaving them wanting more. What's next? No plans for new music except for producing other artists. He feels this work is the best he could possibly obtain given the endless years of working diligently. He dedicates the book to his friends and family who have been very much supportive.

Through The Eyes Of A Killer

The plot here is murder for profit. May it be snuff films, secret agent assassinations or organized crime hits. You'll be wondering what the 4 main protagonist; Axel, Jennifer, Vic and Louie will do next. Some may find some of the content disturbing, snuff films aren't pretty. There is deep insight into what goes into becoming a secret agent. The organized crime first person perspective is a section of the book you may never forget. All of the stars will leave you with a feeling that these are troubled people. Horror and adventure become one here. You'll probably find yourself either loving or hating with a passion each character. It was meant to be that way. Join them on their journey into dark places where they all see no problem killing someone. Will they all survive to live another day? Purchase
