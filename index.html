About

Producer, director, musician and writer. Scott M Atherton has created a tapestry of entertainment that reaches in all directions. His hard work and dedication to nothing but perfection shines in all his endeavours. You should certainly dig deep into his library. Every piece of content, a work of art. His passion is driven and vastly apparent. So go ahead,take your time, explore this site and all it offers. Enter into his mind. See you on the other side.

Music

15 albums made since 2013, amazing. Here are a few key albums, Stoner For Life the newest, proceeded by Emotional Badass and Pilot The Dune, both double albums.Then the classic Fallin’. Full of incredible collaborations along with solo tracks, many say it’s his best. Feel free to click around and listen to some.

Movies

As a multi diverse entertainer Buz has even given film a run. The dr. Benway movies have been a success, there is a trilogy and two promos starring dr. Benway. He should be feared, these movies show you a very dark man who preys on his patients as a psychiatrist. Written, directed, produced, and edited by Buz.

Podcast
