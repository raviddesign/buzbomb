Buz Bomb

Born in 1967, raised in Deer Park, NY. It was 3rd grade and Buz was playing the viola in the school orchestra, he quickly moved on to drums and at age 10 started playing guitar. Immediatly writing original material. In 1984 the first band emerged, FALL OUT. This band led to the hair metal band MASQUERADE, who in the same year transformed to a speed/death/thrash band called VIOLENT AGGRESSION. Popularity high these were good times for Buz. But VIOLENT AGGRESSION split and from the ashes came RENEGADE TRIP, a jam band. Buz worked on many side projects to fill the void in other genres. In 1992 RENEGADE TRIP called it quits and the rap duo of BIG HOOBIE D & BUZ BOMB were born. 4 years in Buz went solo. 1996 and 1999 where the only time any music had been created. But in 2013 Buz came back to the scene and released over 10 albums, and the entire catalog became available online. Booking and promoting shows came Buz's way. And producing music for up and coming artists. Buz began a radio show, created 2 webseries's one 80 episodes strong. Filmmaking then came into focus and Buz created a horror trilogy about a character named Dr. Benway. This psychotic killer sought after supernatural abilities to enter his victims dreams and then murder them the next day. The trilogy is a testamnet to the hard work and creative input Buz is capable of producing and directing without the assistance of anyone, except the actors. Buz is also an author and has a published book THEY CALL HER CHRISTINA and the follow up RAIN OF TERROR is now half way written, will it be published we'll see. There also is a 6 part horror screenplay written. Immersed in all he does Buz is a true multi faceted talent who seems to shine no matter the direction he takes in the entertainment industry. His 250 videos can be found on you tube, and his entire catalog of music on bandcamp. Focused, determined, talented, unstoppable. Buz has had one hell of a career, and it seems it's just begun.
